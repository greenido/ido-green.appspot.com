So Why Should I Use Instinct?

  • Guided Lessons

    No more fumbling through YouTube videos or guitar tab. Our lessons break down each song and teach it step-by-step.

  • Real Songs

    We teach you real music right away, rather than exercises. You’ll be able to play your first song in about 10 minutes.

  • Instant Feedback

    We listen as you play, showing you each correct note and giving you gentle corrections when you make a mistake.

  • No Pressure

    You can move as quickly or as slowly as you like through each lesson. As you play, we adjust to your pace.

  • No Downloads

    Our lessons are entirely web-based and work in any modern browser. There’s nothing to download or install.
